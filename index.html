Harry Potter Hallmark ornaments



Hallmark has recently released their new line of 2021 Hallmark Christmas ornaments.  They have a wide array of ornaments to satisfy everyone’s interests.  This year there is again several Harry Potter Christmas ornaments.  You can push the button on the Harry Potter Sorting Hat ornament and relive the Sorting Ceremony at the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. This Christmas tree ornament features the famous Hogwarts Sorting Hat, which moves and says lines about each house: Gryffindor, Slytherin, Ravenclaw and Hufflepuff.  This ornament will be a hit with any true Harry Potter fan.

In addition to the Sorting Hat there is a Hallmark ornament of the Three Broomsticks which is the cozy village pub in Hogsmeade where Hogwarts students to enjoy drinks during the school year.  There is also a new ornament that feature the book of Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire.  Another Harry Potter ornament celebrates the 20th anniversary of Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone with the Christmas tree ornament features the boy gazing upon the Sorcerer's Stone, which magically appeared in his pocket.  There is even an ornament intended for a newborn baby.  This Christmas ornament is design as a mobile for the little wizard in training.  The mobile features gold charms of Hedwig the Owl, the Sorting Hat, a broomstick, the Golden Snitch and a banner which read Wizard in Training 2021.  So you can start your new baby off with the tales of Harry Potter and his friends with this cute Christmas ornament.

Hallmark has been producing Harry Potter Christmas ornaments since 2000.  There are several different characters to choose from.  Hallmark even produced a Hogwart’s Castle Tree topper that was issued in 2019 and 2020.  This amazing tree topper lights up and it works with the storyteller ornaments from Hallmark.  The tree topper even plays the musical theme for Hedwig from Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone.  The characters of Harry Potter, Ron Weasely, Hermione Granger, Rubeus Hagrid and Albus Dumbledore interact and perform various scenes from Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone.  Not all the characters are required to interact with tree topper.  Each addition Hallmark storyteller ornament adds a different scene to display.  You can gradually add to your collection and unlock more features of the storyteller display.  A good selection of the past Harry Potter Christmas ornaments can be found at The Ornament Shop website.

The earliest Harry Potter ornaments that Hallmark produced were made in pewter for the years of 2000 and 2001.  These ornaments each came in packaging shaped like a book.  Later Hallmark started producing Christmas ornaments representing Harry Potter characters depicting different scenes from the books.  The Harry Potter collection was very popular and so Hallmark added ornaments that depicted various buildings that appear in the J.K. Rowling novels.

Hallmark carries a complete selection of Christmas ornaments.  No matter if you are looking for a Christmas ornament to celebrate a baby’s first Christmas or the various milestones that occur in our lives, Hallmark can satisfy the needs for Christmas ornaments.  These Hallmark ornaments Keepsake ornaments make a great way to remember and cherish the special moments in our lives.  Each year as you decorate your Christmas tree and the memories of the past will be relived and the loved ones remembered.  Start your Hallmark tradition today.
